About Us

Servitude Services Trading (SST) is a 100% black owned level 1 BBBEE contributor company and offers the following core services that form the pillar of the company: Accounting and Taxation, Internal Audit, Supply and Data Analysis.

Servitude Services Trading was initiated out of the need to offer reliable and satisfactory services that will help our clients meet their business end goals. Servitude, meaning the state of being a slave or completely subject to someone.

SST Accounting and Tax is thus a division of SST whose focus is on the provision of both accounting and across the board tax services.

At SST we embrace technology and innovation hence the system we use is compatible to most of the accounting systems used as we understand the importance of meeting customer needs and expectations in an effective and efficient manner as we aim to building long term relationships, making us unique from our counter parts
